Call for Participation

We welcome high-quality submissions on privacy, security, safety, and ethics of AI in pervasive environments.

Submission Types

  • Long Papers: up to 6 pages (excluding references)
  • Short Papers: up to 4 pages (excluding references)
  • Review: double-blind review by workshop committee/organizers

Submission Notes

  • Submit via PCS (Precision Conference System)
  • All submissions must be anonymized
  • Accepted papers will appear in adjunct proceedings
